About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU), advised by Prof. Jianliang XU. My research focuses on revolutionizing Electronic Design Automation (EDA) by integrating database and graph techniques into logic synthesis. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical algorithms and practical EDA challenges to significantly improve circuit PPA. I am actively seeking research internship and full-time opportunities in the EDA or related fields.
